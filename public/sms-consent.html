SMS Communication Policy

Job Flow Co uses SMS text messaging to communicate with customers about their lawn care and landscaping service appointments. This page outlines how we collect consent, what messages you can expect, and how to opt out at any time.

‚úÖ How We Obtain Your Consent

By providing your mobile phone number to Job Flow Co when scheduling a service, you are giving us express written consent to send you text messages related to your service appointments.

Your consent is documented in our customer relationship management (CRM) system at the time of service booking. We maintain records of when and how consent was obtained for all customers who receive SMS notifications.

Types of Messages You'll Receive

We only send transactional messages related to your service. We do NOT send marketing or promotional messages unless you separately opt in to those.

1. Appointment Confirmations

"Hi [Name], your lawn service is scheduled for [Date] at [Time]. Reply STOP to opt out."

2. Weather-Related Updates

"Weather update: We've rescheduled your service to [New Date] due to rain. Reply STOP to opt out."

3. Service Completion Notifications

"Service complete! Your lawn has been mowed. Thank you for choosing Job Flow Co. Reply STOP to opt out."

4. Reminder Messages

"Reminder: Your lawn service is tomorrow at [Time]. We'll see you then! Reply STOP to opt out."
Message Frequency: You can expect to receive approximately 1-4 messages per month, depending on your service schedule and weather conditions. Maximum frequency is 1-2 messages per week.

Your Rights & How to Opt Out

üõë Easy Opt-Out Process

You can stop receiving text messages at any time by:

  1. Reply "STOP" to any text message from us
  2. Reply "UNSUBSCRIBE" to any text message
  3. Contact us directly via phone or email to request removal
  4. Update your preferences in your customer portal

Important: Opting out of SMS messages will not affect your service. We will continue to provide your lawn care services as scheduled. You will simply receive appointment confirmations via email or phone call instead.
